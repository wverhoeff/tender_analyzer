# Civil Engineering Consultancy Fee Matrix 2026

This document lists the hourly charge-out rates for Auckland-based consultancy projects during the 2026 calendar year. Use these figures for all fee calculations.

## Standard Hourly Rates

| Role Code | Role Title | Charge-out Rate ($/hr) | Primary Responsibilities |
| :--- | :--- | :---: | :--- |
| TD | Technical Director | 300 | CPEng Sign-off, Expert Peer Review, Risk Mitigation |
| SE | Senior Engineer (CPEng) | 250 | Principal Design, Calculations, Consents Management |
| IE | Intermediate Civil Engineer | 180 | Detail Design, Catchment Analysis, Reporting |
| DR | Civil 3D Drafter / Modeler | 140 | Drafting, Corridor Modeling, Surface Volume Calculations |

## Direct Cost Markups
* Sub-consultants (e.g. Geotechnical, Survey): Cost + 10%
* Travel expenses: Standard mileage rates apply.
