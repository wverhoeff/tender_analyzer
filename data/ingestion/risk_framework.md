# ACENZ & Risk Management Guidelines

This framework outlines the risk requirements and quality gates for professional civil engineering services.

## Producer Statements (PS)
* **PS1 (Design)**: Required for all structures and earthworks requiring building consents. Must be signed by a Chartered Professional Engineer (CPEng).
* **PS2 (Design Review)**: Mandatory for high-consequence structures or complex hydraulic designs (e.g. retaining walls > 3m high or culverts under public roads).
* **PS4 (Construction Review)**: Ensures the design was executed in accordance with construction plans. Requires on-site observation by a CPEng or representative intermediate engineer.

## ACENZ Liability Limitation
* **Standard Cap**: The maximum liability of the consultancy must be explicitly capped.
* **Liability Capping Formula**: Unless otherwise agreed, cap total liability at **5 times the professional services fee** or **$200,000**, whichever is greater.
* **Limitation Window**: Claims must be raised within **6 years** of contract completion (standard NZ limitation period).

## Auckland Council Units & Unitary Plan Consents
* Earthworks exceeding **250m³** or retaining walls within **1.5m of a boundary** require building and/or resource consent under Auckland Unitary Plan rules. Ensure design scopes incorporate sufficient hours to prepare council forms.
